Our Story

"We do not make watches. We sculpt time."

Founder, Solowave
25+Years of Mastery
180Artisans
12K+Timepieces Crafted

Born in the ateliers of Geneva, Solowave represents the pinnacle of horological artistry. Every component is hand-finished by master craftsmen who dedicate their lives to the pursuit of perfection.

We source only the finest materials 18-karat gold, grade-5 titanium, sapphire crystal, and movements assembled under microscopes. Each watch takes over 400 hours to complete.
